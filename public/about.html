    • About Auto-Mobile Mechanic Locator System

    Empowering Vehicle Owners with Smart, Reliable, and Instant Auto Care

    The Auto-Mobile Mechanic Locator System is an advanced digital platform designed to connect vehicle owners with professional mechanics, spare parts vendors, and roadside assistance providers. Through GPS integration, real-time service requests, and a seamless booking system, the platform ensures fast, efficient, and trustworthy auto care whenever it is needed.

    Why This System Was Developed

    Solving a Common Problem

    Vehicle owners often struggle to find reliable mechanics, especially in emergencies. Long wait times, inconsistent service quality, and lack of transparency have made vehicle maintenance and repairs challenging.

    Bridging the Gap with Technology

    This system eliminates these challenges by providing real-time tracking, verified service providers, and secure payment options, ensuring convenience and quality service on demand.

    A Marketplace for Spare Parts

    Sourcing genuine spare parts can be both time-consuming and expensive. The Spare Parts Marketplace within the system connects users to verified vendors, allowing them to compare prices, check product availability, and receive recommendations from mechanics.

    Key Features and Benefits

    Find and Connect with Skilled Mechanics

    • Instantly locate nearby certified and verified mechanics.
    • Filter search results based on expertise, ratings, and availability.
    • Book appointments and access a wide range of repair services.

    Appointment and Booking System

    • Schedule an appointment with a mechanic at a repair shop or request service at a specified location.
    • Receive real-time notifications for confirmed bookings and appointment reminders.
    • Manage past service records and reschedule appointments easily.

    Live GPS Tracking and Navigation

    • Track the mechanic’s arrival in real time.
    • Use integrated maps to locate the nearest repair shop.
    • Get an estimated arrival time for better planning.

    On-Demand Roadside Assistance

    • Request emergency services such as towing, battery jump-starts, tire changes, and fuel delivery.
    • Utilize the SOS feature for immediate breakdown assistance.

    In-App Communication and Support

    • Directly chat or call mechanics for consultations.
    • AI-powered chatbot provides quick troubleshooting tips.
    • Customer support is available for service disputes and refund requests.

    Spare Parts Marketplace

    • Purchase spare parts directly from trusted vendors.
    • Compare prices, check product ratings, and view availability.
    • Mechanics can recommend and order parts necessary for vehicle repairs.

    Flexible Payment Options

    • Secure transactions through mobile payments, online transfers, and credit/debit card options.
    • Option for cash payments upon service completion.
    • Payment-splitting options available for large repair costs.

    AI-Powered Vehicle Diagnostics and Self-Help Guide

    • AI-driven diagnostics tool allows users to enter vehicle symptoms and receive possible issue predictions.
    • Access self-help repair guides for minor fixes such as oil changes and fuse replacements.

    Mechanic Rating and Review System

    • Customers can rate mechanics based on service quality, reliability, and professionalism.
    • Highly-rated mechanics are prioritized in search results.
    • A ranking system highlights top-performing mechanics for greater user confidence.

    Loyalty Program and Discounts

    • Earn reward points for bookings and referrals.
    • Access exclusive discounts on spare parts and maintenance services.
    • Discounted packages available for regular vehicle servicing.
